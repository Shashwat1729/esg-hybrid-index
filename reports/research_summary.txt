IMPORTANT DATA PROVENANCE NOTE
======================================================================

This research uses a hybrid ESG dataset combining three data tiers:
  1. REAL: Yahoo Finance governance risk scores (audit, board,
     compensation, shareholder rights risk) for all 276+ companies
  2. REAL: SEC EDGAR XBRL filings (R&D, employees, board size)
     for ~77 US companies
  3. PROXY-DERIVED: 12 financial-proxy-based ESG indicators with
     documented academic rationale, calibrated within sectors

Environmental and social pillar indicators are predominantly
proxy-derived. Governance indicators are predominantly real.
All provenance is tracked at per-company, per-indicator level.
Statistical significance of ESG-financial relationships should
be interpreted as reflecting the proxy construction methodology.
